Projects

A Deep Learning Framework for Multi-Speaker Video Dubbing Using Whisper, NLLB and Wav2Lip

On Progress......

This project presents an AI-driven automated video dubbing system that enables seamless multilingual content generation from single-language source videos. The system focuses on preserving natural lip synchronization and maintaining character-specific voice modulation to enhance the realism and immersion of dubbed content. By integrating state-of-the-art AI models, the platform ensures high-quality translation and dubbing across multiple languages. The architecture leverages Whisper (OpenAI) for accurate speech recognition, NLLB (Meta) for efficient and context-aware translation, Text-to-Speech (TTS) systems for dynamic and customizable voice generation, and Wav2Lip for realistic lip-syncing. These components are orchestrated through a robust backend powered by FastAPI, which handles all AI inference processes.
